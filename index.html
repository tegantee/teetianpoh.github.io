01 / FINAL YEAR PROJECT · 2026

Concealed Weapon Detection — Multi-modal Sensor System

NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano · FLIR Lepton 3.5 · Acconeer XM125 Radar

Built a real-time concealed weapon detection pipeline fusing a 60 GHz radar sensor with a thermal camera and a YOLOv8 TensorRT model. The radar provides binary presence detection and computes a Region of Interest that gates and crops YOLO inference, reducing unnecessary compute on the edge device. A multithreaded C++ SPI/I2C capture pipeline feeds thermal frames through OpenCV preprocessing before inference. A Kalman filter smooths radar distance estimates for stable cross-frame target tracking.

C++ Python YOLOv8 TensorRT OpenCV Kalman Filter 60GHz Radar Thermal Imaging Edge AI
02 / IC DESIGN · 2025

CMOS Circuit Design & Analysis

Cadence Virtuoso · Fundamental of IC Design Lab

Designed and simulated CMOS inverter and logic gate circuits in Cadence Virtuoso. Performed DC sweep, transient, and AC analyses. Investigated MOSFET sizing (W/L ratio) effects on VTC, switching threshold, noise margins, and propagation delay. Achieved targeted threshold voltages by tuning PMOS/NMOS width ratios.
